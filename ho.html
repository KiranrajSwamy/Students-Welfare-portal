SCOLARSHIP SCHEME FOR
THE MERITIOUS STUDENTS AND HANDICAPPED STUDENT

GEOGRAPHIC BOUNDRY
urban and rural area of pune district

objectives

  • Ensuring educational development of meritorious students,free from
    economic restraints of low income group families.
  • Carry forword the process of consistent and continous development of
    potential talents.

TARGET COMMUNITY
300 students anually are to be given scholarship.

ELIGIBILITY OF SCHOLARSHIP

  • Must be citizen of India.
  • Must be permanent resident of Pune or continously living in Pune for
    the last 5 years.
  • To apply:-
    Under Graduate Level
  • 80% entry level percentage
    Post graduate level
  • 80% entry level percentage for non professional courses(science,
    commerse,arts etc.)
  • 5% grace in percentage for student reappying in succesive years
    both professional and non-professional courses will be admissable.
  • maximim age for scholarship at graduate level shall be 22 years and
    for scholarship at post-graduate level shall be 25 years.
  • one child of family shall be given scholarship in one acdemic years.
