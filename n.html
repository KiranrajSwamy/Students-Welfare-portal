Terms and condition for Undergraduate and Postgraduate. scholarship :-

 For Undergraduate students :
A : Age limit : 22 Years.
B : Family's total annual income not to exceed Rs. 2,00,000/-
C : Candidate should secured 80% marks in last examination.
G : The scholarship amount will be decided on merit and need but will not exceed Rs. 10,000/- per year
D :students having gap in education from any streams are not eligible to apply for this scholarship.
E : The scholarship is for a period of only one academic years .  
For Postgraduate students
A : Age limit : 25 Years.
B : Family's total annual income not to exceed Rs. 2,00,000/- per annum.
C : Candidate should have  secured a minimum of 80% marks in last examination
D : Only those who are pursuing postgraduation studies in the 1st year of Science and doing specialization with Mathematics, Statistics, Physics & Computer Science / Application can apply.
E : The scholarship amount will be decided on merit and need but will not exceed Rs. 20,000/- per year.
F : The scholarship is for a period of one academic year.

- The decision of the Board of Trustees will be binding and final and amount of the application form is non-refundable
- Payment can be deposite in account number given in application form.
